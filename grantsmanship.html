I completed the Center for Communication Excellence (CCE) Grantsmanship Summer Intensive at Iowa State University, a self-paced microcredential program covering strategies for identifying funding opportunities, developing competitive research narratives, and effective grant writing.

NSF CDS&E Proposal Submission Submitted
