About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the Department of Aerospace Engineering at Iowa State University, Ames, IA. My research is centered on turbulence modeling, generative learning, and large language models.

I received my Ph.D. in Mechanics from the School of Aerospace Engineering, Tsinghua University (2024). Before joining Iowa State University, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the Department of Mathematics, University of Texas at Arlington (2024–2025), where I developed advanced vortex identification methods and proposed new subgrid-scale models for large eddy simulations.

Only a profound understanding of the underlying physics of turbulence can pave the way for truly effective turbulence modeling.

I serve as a reviewer for Physics of Fluids, Aerospace Science and Technology, and International Journal of Heat and Fluid Flow.
